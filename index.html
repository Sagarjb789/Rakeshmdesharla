Welcome to our beloved leader website

Rakesh M Desharla

State Working Vice President

Rakesh was born as a child of Jain parents, at Maddur Mandya district on Septeber 5th 1988, and he is basically from Rajasthan. His father is Madan Raj Jain and Mother is Santhosh devi. Rakesh is the Eldest Son.
Earlier education started at St. Anne's Convent Maddur. Later further studies completed in the PES College at Mandya.
After his Studeis, he was actively Assisting to his father at his business.
As he is respectful towards his school every year he celebrates his birthday at his school with his teachers, in a unique manner. it is the example for his simplicity.
​ As time permitted him to start his life by entering to Politics. He joined JDS , and became a State General Secreatery by his own unique qualities.
He actively participated in the Political activities and helped a people in his own identified way by conducting eye operation campaigns and blood donating campaigns and by helping to Daliths. He is very dedicated towards Social Service.
